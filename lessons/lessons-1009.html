Prerequisite Knowledge

  • Warming up, warming down, physical training, aerobic dance, flexibility, yoga, weight training, physical fitness, sports and games from high school.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the importance of sound health and fitness principles.
  • Expose students to physical and yogic activities for health and fitness.
  • Create a safe, progressive and efficient activity plan to reduce injury risk.
  • Develop appreciation of physical activity as a lifetime pursuit.
