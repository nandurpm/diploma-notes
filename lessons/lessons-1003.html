Quick Revision Sheet

Last-minute exam checklist and common mistakes.

Memory tricks
  • v-u-at: final velocity = initial velocity + acceleration effect.
  • F-ma: more mass needs more force for same acceleration.
  • v-rω: rim speed increases with radius.
  • PE → KE: falling body converts stored energy into motion energy.
  • Av constant: narrow pipe gives faster flow.
Common mistakes
  • Writing answer without SI unit.
  • Not converting cm, mm, rpm or minutes to SI.
  • Adding vectors like scalars.
  • Confusing mass and weight.
  • Using gauge pressure where absolute pressure is needed.
Before exam
  • Can I define every key term?
  • Can I write formulas from memory?
  • Can I solve at least five numerical problems?
  • Can I draw important diagrams neatly?
  • Did I revise answer keys?
