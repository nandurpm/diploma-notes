Mini Formula Bank

Mini Formula Bank for Mathematics I

Use this as the last-minute memory page before exams and practical sessions.

Modulus of complex number

|a+bi| = √(a²+b²)

Where used: Complex numbers

Common mistake: Do not forget square root.

Conjugate

Conjugate of a+bi is a-bi

Where used: Complex arithmetic

Common mistake: Only sign of imaginary part changes.

Slope form

y - y₁ = m(x - x₁)

Where used: Straight line equation

Common mistake: Use correct point and slope.

Angle between lines

tan θ = |(m₂-m₁)/(1+m₁m₂)|

Where used: Coordinate geometry

Common mistake: If denominator is zero, lines are perpendicular.

Radian conversion

θ rad = θ° × π/180

Where used: Trigonometry

Common mistake: Calculator mode matters.

Identity

sin²A + cos²A = 1

Where used: Simplification

Common mistake: Square applies to ratio, not angle.

Derivative power rule

d/dx(xⁿ)=n xⁿ⁻¹

Where used: Differentiation

Common mistake: Reduce power by one.

Product rule

(uv)' = u'v + uv'

Where used: Differentiation

Common mistake: Do not multiply derivatives only.

Quotient rule

(u/v)'=(vu' - uv')/v²

Where used: Differentiation

Common mistake: Order of numerator signs matters.

Chain rule

dy/dx = dy/du × du/dx

Where used: Composite functions

Common mistake: Differentiate inner function too.

Parametric derivative

dy/dx = (dy/dt)/(dx/dt)

Where used: Parametric equations

Common mistake: Denominator cannot be zero.
