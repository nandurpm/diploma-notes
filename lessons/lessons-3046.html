Master Answer Key

Answers

M1-Q1: 555, 565, LM380 and 7490.
M1-Q2: Check pin number, supply voltage, polarity and ground before powering the circuit.
M2-Q1: Aim: construct AM generator and observe modulation.
M2-Q2: Diode rectifies AM signal and filter recovers message.
M2-Q3: 565 PLL locks to FM input and gives demodulated output.
M3-Q1: PAM varies amplitude, PWM varies width and PPM varies position.
M3-Q2: Apply message and sampling signal, observe sampled pulses and recover output.
M4-Q1: Pre-emphasis boosts high frequency components; de-emphasis restores response and reduces noise.
M4-Q2: Mixer combines signals to produce sum and difference frequency components.
