Mini Formula Bank

Mini Formula Bank for Communication Skills in English

Use this as the last-minute memory page before exams and practical sessions.

Skimming

Reading quickly to get the main idea.

Where used: Use before detailed reading.

Common mistake: Do not try to understand every word first.

Scanning

Searching quickly for specific information.

Where used: Use for dates, names, values and keywords.

Common mistake: Avoid reading the entire passage unnecessarily.

Concord

Subject and verb must agree in number/person.

Where used: Grammar correction and sentence writing.

Common mistake: Singular subject needs singular verb.

Context clue

Nearby words help guess unfamiliar meanings.

Where used: Reading comprehension vocabulary.

Common mistake: Do not guess outside the passage.

Formal email

Subject, salutation, purpose, details, closing and signature.

Where used: Workplace communication.

Common mistake: Avoid slang and long unclear sentences.

Graph report

Describe trend, comparison and conclusion objectively.

Where used: Technical report writing.

Common mistake: Do not write personal opinions as data.
