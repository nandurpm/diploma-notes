About Diploma Notes

One organized study portal for Kerala Polytechnic students.

Find syllabus documents, student-friendly lessons, notes, model question papers and scheme-wise learning materials without searching across many websites.

Our Purpose

Spend more time studying and less time searching.

Diploma Notes is an independent educational resource created to make Kerala Polytechnic study materials easier to locate and use. Subjects are arranged by revision, department, semester and course code so students can quickly move from the official syllabus to lessons, notes and question-paper resources.

The portal is designed for mobile phones, tablets and desktop computers. It can be used for regular study, quick revision, assignment reference, exam preparation and checking the official course structure.

How to Use Diploma Notes

A simple three-step study route

Use the portal in this order to avoid opening the wrong syllabus revision or subject.

1

Select the correct scheme

Open Revision 2021 for current REV2021 subjects. Use 2015 Materials only when you specifically need the older scheme.

2

Choose department and semester

Open your department, select the semester, and verify the course code and title before opening a resource.

3

Study and verify

Start with the syllabus, continue with the lesson or notes, and use model question papers for exam practice. Confirm important academic details on the official SITTTR website.

Important academic notice

Diploma Notes is an independent study-support project and is not the official SITTTR or Government of Kerala website. Materials are organized for easier learning, but students should use official SITTTR publications, institution notices and examination-board instructions as the final authority for syllabus, examination pattern, marks, dates and regulations.
